Lionel Messi Information Document
Personal Details:
Full Name: Lionel Andrés Messi
Date of Birth: June 24, 1987
Place of Birth: Rosario, Argentina
Nationality: Argentine
Height: 1.70m (5 ft 7 in)
Weight: 72 kg (159 lbs)
Position: Forward
Current Club: Paris Saint-Germain F.C. (PSG)
Previous Club: FC Barcelona
Early Life:
Lionel Messi was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina. From a young age, he displayed exceptional talent and passion for football. At the age of 13, Messi relocated to Barcelona, Spain, to join the FC Barcelona youth academy, La Masia, after being offered a contract by the club.

Career Highlights:
FC Barcelona (2004-2021): Messi spent the majority of his professional career at FC Barcelona, where he became the club's all-time leading scorer. He won numerous domestic and international titles, including:
10 La Liga titles
7 Copa del Rey titles
4 UEFA Champions League titles
7 Ballon d'Or awards (as of 2021)
Paris Saint-Germain F.C. (2021-present): Messi joined PSG in August 2021, signing a contract after leaving FC Barcelona due to financial constraints. He continues to showcase his exceptional talent with the French club.
International Career:
Argentina National Team: Messi has represented Argentina in numerous international tournaments, including the FIFA World Cup and the Copa América. Despite facing criticism for not winning a major international trophy earlier in his career, Messi helped lead Argentina to victory in the 2021 Copa América, earning his first major international title.
Playing Style:
Messi is renowned for his exceptional dribbling skills, close ball control, vision, and precise finishing.
He is often praised for his ability to change the course of a game single-handedly and his knack for scoring crucial goals in key moments.
Records and Achievements:
Messi holds numerous records in football, including the most goals scored in a calendar year (91 goals in 2012) and the most Ballon d'Or awards.
He is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time, with his name often mentioned alongside other legends like Pelé and Diego Maradona.
Personal Life:
Messi is married to Antonela Roccuzzo, his childhood sweetheart, with whom he has three children: Thiago, Mateo, and Ciro.
Outside of football, Messi is involved in various philanthropic activities through his Leo Messi Foundation, which aims to provide opportunities for vulnerable children and communities worldwide.
Endorsements:
Messi has numerous endorsements with top brands such as Adidas, Pepsi, and Huawei, making him one of the highest-paid athletes in the world.
